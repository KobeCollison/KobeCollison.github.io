Two Finals in One

For my advanced geometry class, our final project is to imagine a world that applies by a different geometry to ours. We would also have to fabricate a model of some aspect or artifact of the world. Because there was so much overlap with this and my digifab final. The professors allowed me do both projects in one, A model of a world with a unique geometry, with a model fabricated using multiple digital fabrication methods.

Artifact: The Hedron

as explained here, my fabrication is to create a model of the rollercoaster, The Hedron The Hedron is a rollercoaster that takes advantage of the fact that it is inside of a 3-manifold, in this case being a Cube with each opposite side connected so that passing through one will result in comming out other. We will be using Acrylic and wood to make the box that contains the ride, Along with 3D printed rails (FDM Printer) to represent the tracks with signs and other decorations like the cart made with laser cutting and more 3D printing. Finally, to the tracks would have a porgrammed LED light strip accross them to represent the cart's movement.

My part: Digital Lab: programming LED strips

The first part was designing the cirtcuit in a program called ciCad, which I honestly have no idea how to do at the moment, so I had the help of someone who works at the lab, who helped me through a large amount of this entire project

I used a machine to cut out the copper circuitry.

Then I used solder to attach the pins, button, capacitor, resister and controller. The solder was a liquid paste at first, but after being heated by a heat gun, it cooled into a solid form, attaching the components.

Then comes programming the chip in ardruino. The idea is that when the button is pressed, a light travels throughout all of the neopixels 1 at a time to represent the cart moving throughout the track. I added a few extra things though. For one, every time the button is pressed the cart has a random color.

Also, we measured how many neopixels there were on each strip so I could color the lights at the end of each strip to symbolize where the cart would jump to next after reaching the end of one strip.
