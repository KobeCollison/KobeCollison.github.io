Week 5: CNC Routing

Project: Make a Desk Organizer

This week, our project was to make a desk organizer. The organizer needed an outlined circle and a minimum of 3 slots for our personal objects and text

Designing in Fusion360

The first part of this process would be to design the shapes we will be carving out of the wood and the specific dimensions of the objects we are working with. We used a sketch in Fusion360.

1. This box represents the wood plank that the organizer will be cut out of.

2. This rounded rectangle is the actual desk organizer.

3. The pot holder with a 3 inch diameter

4. The holder for the box that I made in my laser cutting assignment.

5. a place to put pens and pencils

6. an area for my earphones case.

7. now that the sketch is complete, we can extrude parts of it to get an image of what the desk organizer will look like

8. I will extrude by 3/4 inches as that is the depth of the wood

9. for the pocket cuts, I extrude by only half an inch.

10. for the pot holder, since that is an exterior cut, part of it should be partially extruded.
